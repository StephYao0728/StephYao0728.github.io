Pinghe Math Club

Introduction

Pinghe Math Club is an academic club totally run by students in Shanghai Pinghe School. It is dedicated to studying and sharing mathematical knowledge. Led by students who have a passion for math, the club holds weekly activities in the form of lectures. The aim of the club is to enhance students' interest in math, foster mutual help in mathematical learning, and promote a vibrant mathematical atmosphere within the school.
